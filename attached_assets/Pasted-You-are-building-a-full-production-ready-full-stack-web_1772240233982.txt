🔵 COST ESTIMATION ENGINE

Base logic:

Parts Cost = based on detected parts
Paint Cost = based on affected panels
Labor = 30% of parts cost

🔵 DEDUCTIBLE LOGIC

If Comprehensive:
Deductible = ₹1000 OR 5% of total (whichever higher)

If Third Party:
Insurance pays ₹0

Calculate:

Total Estimate
– Deductible
= Insurance Payable

Customer Payable = Deductible

🔵 AI APPROVAL LOGIC
